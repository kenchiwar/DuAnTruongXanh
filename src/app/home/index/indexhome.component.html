About us

KENTUCKY is a utility website application, built on the Intranet, allowing school staff, lecturers and students to access and use throughout the campus. This system has helped to automate the workflow and efficiently deal with service requests related

With diverse integration features, KENTUCKY covers various types of campus facilities such as classrooms, laboratories, dormitories, buckets, canteens, gymnasiums, computer centers, fishing faculty clubs, and many other institutions. This helps to ensure that any service requests from users are handled conveniently and accurately.

Utilities

KENTUCKY brings significant and special utilities to users, helping them to experience a convenient and useful way.

Easy to use interface

With a user-friendly interface and diverse integration, KENTUCKY allows users to easily access and use features, helping them make the most of the opportunities and services in the school.

Solve quickly and accurately

KENTUCKY assists in resolving service requests quickly and accurately, ensuring any issues are handled efficiently and reliably.

Optimizing use of facilities

KENTUCKY helps to effectively manage the use of facilities such as classrooms, laboratories, dormitories, etc., thereby optimizing scheduling and resource usage in the school.
